# 🌸 Ugadi Pachadi Fortune

A festive interactive web experience built for **Ugadi** — the Telugu & Kannada New Year.

Based on the ancient tradition of tasting **Ugadi Pachadi**, a dish made of six flavours that symbolise the full spectrum of life — sweet, sour, salty, bitter, spicy, and astringent — this site lets you "take the first bite" and discover what your year holds.

## ✨ Features
- Animated marigold garlands (తోరణం) and clay pot illustration
- Click to stir the Pachadi and reveal your fortune
- Six taste fortunes with Telugu names and meanings
- Share your result with friends
- Fully self-contained — single HTML file, no dependencies

## 🎨 Design
Inspired by traditional Telugu festival aesthetics — deep brown backgrounds, golden marigolds, and hand-illustrated clay pottery with white decorative patterns.

## 📖 About Ugadi Pachadi
The six tastes of Ugadi Pachadi represent the mixed experiences of life:
| Taste | Telugu | Meaning |
|-------|--------|---------|
| Sweet | తీపి | Joy, warmth, happiness |
| Sour | పులుపు | Change, surprise, adaptation |
| Salty | ఉప్పు | Steadiness, realism, everyday life |
| Bitter | చేదు | Hard lessons, perspective, growth |
| Spicy | కారం | Intensity, ambition, courage |
| Astringent | వగరు | Reflection, uncertainty, pause |
